AI Content Use Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Our Commitment to Transparency

At comparellms, we believe in being transparent about how we create our content. This policy explains how we use AI tools in our content creation process and our commitment to accuracy and safety.

How We Use AI

comparellms publishes comparisons that are produced with the assistance of AI tools and human review. We use AI (including various LLMs like GPT, Gemini, Claude, and others) to:

  • Gather and synthesize research about different AI models
  • Draft initial comparison frameworks
  • Generate structured data and benchmark summaries
  • Assist with formatting and organization

Human Review Process

Every published article undergoes the following review process:

  1. Fact-checking: All claims about model capabilities are verified against official documentation and reputable sources
  2. Editorial review: A human editor reviews all content for accuracy, clarity, and fairness
  3. Bias check: We ensure comparisons are balanced and don't unfairly favor any particular model or company
  4. Final approval: No content is published without human approval

What We Mark as AI-Assisted

  • Drafts that include model outputs are reviewed and corrected before publication
  • We cite sources where applicable and include summary notes
  • Technical specifications are verified against official documentation
  • Benchmark data is sourced from reputable testing organizations or official sources

Content Standards

All content published on comparellms adheres to the following standards:

  • Accuracy: Information is verified and up-to-date
  • Objectivity: Comparisons are fair and unbiased
  • Clarity: Technical concepts are explained accessibly
  • Attribution: Sources are properly cited

Safety and Moderation

We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful platform:

  • We do not publish content that violates laws or our safety standards
  • Content that could be harmful, misleading, or discriminatory is not published
  • We follow responsible AI guidelines in our content creation

Copyright and Citations

We respect intellectual property rights:

  • AI-generated content is reviewed for potential copyright issues
  • We cite original sources and give credit where due
  • Screenshots and logos are used under fair use for comparison purposes

Corrections and Updates

If you find an error in our content:

  • We welcome corrections and will update articles promptly
  • Significant corrections are noted in the article
  • Updated dates are displayed on revised articles

Report a Concern

If you find problematic content on our site or have concerns about our AI use:

  1. Email us at contact@rankllms.com
  2. Describe the issue and provide the article URL
  3. We will review and respond within 48 hours

Questions?

If you have questions about this policy or how we use AI, please email us at contact@rankllms.com or visit our contact page. We're happy to discuss our processes and address any concerns.
